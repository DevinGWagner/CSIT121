CoD(Call of Duty)

Call of Duty is one of the longest running FPS games on the market at this time. The first game of the series came out in 2003 and has maintained being one of the largest as well as oldest FPS in the market. They have adapted with the times and started there own Battle Royale called Warzone.

PUBG(PlayerUnknown BattleGrounds)

The game combines elements of exploration, tactical decision-making, and skilled marksmanship, challenging players to stay alive while eliminating enemies to become the last person or team standing. Whether playing solo, with a partner, or in a squad, PUBG offers a thrilling and competitive experience where survival and skill determine the ultimate victor.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a FPS as well as a BattleRoyale so it will fit into both categories. This game came out in 2019 and was off to a strong start with the prospect of a Pro League scene that was inevitably slowed drastically by COVID-19.
